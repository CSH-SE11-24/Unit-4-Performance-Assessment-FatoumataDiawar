About Us!

Located in the soundview area of the Bronx, Daly BLP was a buisness I founded years ago when I was at the time, in a foreign country raising my daughter on my own. I was lost, and did some hair braiding in some salons in order to provide for me and my daugher. After some time, I began to find joy in what I do, and wanted to be able to bring quality service to my clients. So I made the decision to start a new buisness on my own. Daly BLP is one of my own children, and I"m thankful for all it's done for me and my family. I hope to see you in the salon soon! - Daly
